BAT battle -- Chinese cloud platforms comparison.

Recent years, several Chinese companies have started their cloud computing platforms. There are three famous internet companies in China named BAT, which stands for Baidu, Alibaba, and Tencent.

Baidu is the leading Chinese language Internet search provider, and it also contains various internet-related services and AI techniques. The advanced technology and a large number of users provide it with strong support to establish their cloud platform. Alibaba is another tech giant in China. It is the biggest online commerce company in China, hosting millions of merchants and it has more than eight hundred million of users, so its cloud service is obviously also very competitive in China. Tencent is world’s largest gaming company and is also one of the world’s largest social media company, so based on its solid fundamentals and rich experience in various internet areas, Tencent Cloud has developed a really comprehensive and mature service system.
